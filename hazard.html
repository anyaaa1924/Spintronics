Burns

Cool the burn with running water for 10 minutes. Do not apply creams or pop blisters. Cover with a clean cloth.

Bleeding

Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth. Elevate the area if possible. Seek help if bleeding doesn't stop.

CPR

Push hard and fast in the center of the chest (100-120 bpm). Give rescue breaths if trained. Call emergency services.

Fractures

Immobilize the area. Do not move the injured part. Apply ice if available. Get medical help immediately.
